About Us


Supporting Selwyn community since 1979. Lincoln Fitness is the biggest gym in Selwyn, with brand new facilities and equipment available. Offering world-class Les Mills programmes with more than 40 group exercise classes per week.

Our gym is open 24/7, so you can train whenever you want. We have a wide range of cardio and strength equipment, including free weights and resistance machines. We also have a dedicated functional training area, with a range of equipment to help you improve your strength, power, and speed.
